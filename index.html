  • Edge of Space Explorer
    I sent a Raspberry Pi computer to the edge of space using a high-altitude balloon as a primary school's anniversary celebration STEM project. It collected telemetry as it went and sent back live photos of it's journey.
  • Forest Creator
    I planned, planting and nurtured a woodland in Tottenham to enable Forest School sessions in my primary school. It has thrived and is an amazing outdoor learning space that the whole school loves.
  • Primary STEM and Computing Lead
    As a primary STEM and computing lead I taught with high-pressure water rockets, wind powered cars and balloon popping robot wars using robots that the children designed, built and programmed.
  • Forest School Leader
    Being a Forest School Leader was about helping children to stay safe and learn ractical skills that would help them stay safe in the world but was far more about helping them develop into joyful, kind and aspirational people.
  • Furniture Craftsperson
    I studied Furniture Craftsmanship & Design at university in pursuit of my life-long love of making and the desire to bring beautiful things into the world.
